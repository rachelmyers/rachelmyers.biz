RailsBridge

RailsBridge is an organization that teaches marginalized groups Ruby, Rails, and Frontend development.

The RailBridge community and workshops were a huge help to me when I started programming, and I started teaching as soon as I was able. I'm currently on the RailsBridge Board of Directors and volunteer regularly at workshops in San Francisco.

If you'd like to get involved, check out Bridge Troll for a workshop near you, start an issue in our Organizing Repo, or help us maintain the curriculum! Also, checkout ClojureBridge and MobileBridge.
